About Me

I'm a dedicated biostatistician with a passion for leveraging data to improve public health outcomes. At the Department of Population Medicine, I focus on analyzing complex datasets to inform healthcare policies and practices.

My academic journey began at Brown University, where I earned my ScM in Biostatistics. Since then, I've collaborated on numerous research projects, contributing to advancements in genomics and epidemiology.
